Where Plates Are Shared, and Hearts Connect for Good Food, Great Company & Unforgettable Moments.

  • Burgers
    Bite into our juicy, handcrafted burgers made with premium ingredients. Your choice of sauces, each burger is a flavorful delight. From classic chicken patties to veggie options, we promise a hearty, satisfying meal in every bite
  • Pizza
    Indulge in our freshly baked pizza, topped with gooey cheese, flavorful tomato sauce, and your favorite toppings. Our pizza is a perfect harmony of crispy crust and savory goodness that satisfies every craving. A slice of happiness awaits you!
  • Pasta
    Dive into a bowl of our creamy, flavorful pasta that’s cooked to perfection. From rich Alfredo to tangy marinara, every dish is crafted with fresh ingredients and love. Whether you prefer classic spaghetti or penne, our pasta is a comforting delight for your taste buds.
  • Tandoori
    Experience the authentic taste of India with our perfectly spiced tandoori dishes. Marinated in aromatic herbs and cooked in a traditional clay oven, each bite is smoky, tender, and bursting with flavor. From chicken to paneer, enjoy the ultimate culinary indulgence.
  • French Fries
    Enjoy the crispy perfection of our golden French fries, freshly fried to a light crunch. Sprinkled with a hint of salt or your choice of seasonings, they’re the ideal snack or side. Pair them with our zesty dips for an irresistible treat that’s simply addicting!
  • Momos
    Bite into the soft, steamed goodness of our momos, filled with a delightful mix of fresh ingredients and bursting with flavor. Served with a spicy, tangy dipping sauce, these dumplings are perfect for satisfying your cravings. Enjoy a taste of tradition with every bite!
  • Sandwiches
    Our sandwiches are crafted with the freshest ingredients, combining crisp vegetables, tender meats, and delicious spreads. Each bite delivers a perfect balance of flavors, making it an ideal choice for a quick snack or a wholesome meal. Toasted or fresh, they’re made to please!
  • KFC Items
    Experience the iconic taste of KFC with our signature crispy, golden-brown chicken, seasoned with a secret blend of spices. Whether it’s the fiery wings, juicy burgers, or classic fried chicken, every bite is packed with bold, mouthwatering flavors
  • Biryani
    Indulge in the royal flavors of our biryani, where fragrant basmati rice is layered with tender meat or vegetables and an aromatic blend of spices. Each spoonful is a perfect medley of taste and tradition, making it a feast for your senses.
  • Kebab Kanti
    Savor the smoky aroma and succulent taste of our kebab kanti, featuring perfectly grilled chunks of marinated meat. Seasoned with rich spices and grilled to perfection, these kebabs are a true celebration of flavor and texture.
